About page:


More or Less is a transdisciplinary collective based in Zurich, Switzerland, founded by Antonia Orfanou and Victoria Cheredeeva.

Their work combines interaction design principles, performing art and practice-based artistic research. The collective explores the intersections of technology and art enriched by philosophical and scientific perspectives.

Their projects are characterized by a deep curiosity about perception, experience and the boundaries of the visual, blending pop culture references and technological elements. Driven by a love for experimentation, More or Less challenge the expected and creates for the joy of seeing different visual and conceptual worlds come together.
